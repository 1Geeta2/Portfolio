Frequently Asked Questions - VOTS

What is VOTS?
VOTS (Vidyalankar Organs and Tissue Transplantation) is an advanced organ donation platform offering real-time tracking, emergency hospital availability, awareness campaigns, and secure donor-recipient connectivity.
What makes VOTS different?
VOTS provides real-time organ tracking, emergency hospital availability, text-to-speech assistance for the deaf, precise location services, and enhanced donor registry with real-time status tracking.
How do I register as a donor?
To register as a donor, visit our website and complete the donor registration form. Your eligibility will be verified through a medical screening process.
How does real-time tracking work?
Our system tracks organs from the donor to the recipient using a secure GPS-enabled network, ensuring safe and efficient delivery.
Can I check hospital availability?
Yes, VOTS provides real-time hospital availability data, helping recipients locate transplant-ready medical facilities.
What support does VOTS provide?
VOTS offers donor and recipient support, including medical guidance, legal assistance, psychological counseling, and participation in awareness programs.
How does VOTS ensure ethical donation?
VOTS follows strict ethical and legal guidelines to ensure voluntary, safe, and medically approved organ donations.
Is organ donation safe for living donors?
Yes, living organ donation is safe with thorough medical evaluation and post-donation care to ensure the donor's health is not compromised.
What happens if a transplant fails?
If a transplant fails, medical professionals will assess the situation and consider re-transplantation or alternative treatments.
Can I track my donor or recipient?
To protect privacy, donor and recipient identities remain confidential, but case progress can be tracked anonymously.
