First Aid Tips

Click below to view essential first aid tips for common emergencies.

  • CPR: Check breathing, call emergency, and start chest compressions.
  • Burns: Run cool water over the burn, do not apply ice, and cover with a sterile dressing.
  • Bleeding: Apply direct pressure with a clean cloth, elevate the wound, and seek medical help.
  • Fractures: Immobilize the affected area, avoid moving the injured limb, and get medical assistance.
  • Choking: Encourage coughing; if ineffective, perform abdominal thrusts (Heimlich maneuver).
  • Snake Bite: Keep the affected limb still, avoid applying ice or cutting the wound, and seek emergency help.
  • Heat Stroke: Move to a cool place, hydrate with water, and use cool compresses to lower body temperature.
  • Heart Attack: Have the person sit down, loosen tight clothing, and call emergency services immediately.
