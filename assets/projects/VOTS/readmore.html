About Vidyalankar Organ and Tissue Transplantation (VOTS)

Vidyalankar Organ and Tissue Transplantation (VOTS) is a dedicated initiative to create awareness about organ donation and provide a seamless, real-time platform for organ transplantation services. Our mission is to save lives through organized efforts, bringing together donors, recipients, and healthcare providers. Through VOTS, we aim to make organ donation accessible, transparent, and impactful.

Join our mission to save lives and make a difference in the world. Together, we can bring hope to those in need.
