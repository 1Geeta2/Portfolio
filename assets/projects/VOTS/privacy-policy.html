Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2024

Introduction

Welcome to VOTS (Vidyalankar Organ and Tissue Transplantation). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you use our services.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Name and contact details
  • Medical information and history
  • Identification documents (Aadhaar, medical reports)
  • Location data
  • Communication preferences

Usage Data

  • Website interaction data
  • Device information
  • IP addresses
  • Browser type and version

How We Use Your Information

  • To process organ donation and recipient registrations
  • To match donors with recipients
  • To provide medical and emergency services
  • To improve our services
  • To communicate with you about our services
  • To comply with legal obligations

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Secure storage systems
  • Regular security assessments
  • Access controls and authentication

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Opt-out of communications
  • File a complaint about data handling
